About the Author

Pratham Khathuria is a young storyteller based in Hyderabad, Bharat, passionate about imagination, curiosity and the mysteries of the universe. He published his first book at 17, marking the beginning of his journey as a writer exploring stories that inspire young minds and spark creativity. While his debut work introduces children to the excitement of space adventures, future stories may explore many different worlds and ideas.

— Pratham Khathuria
